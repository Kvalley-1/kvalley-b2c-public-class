14. 「對上要扛結果、對下要分工、對外要跨部門協調」— 多篇中文管理文 paraphrase 共識
15. 「我已經把『角色切換』練成肌肉記憶 — 還是會在切到第五次的時候爆炸」— paraphrase

### C 級

## 線 3：「AI 工具卡住了」真實用戶抱怨（11 條，全 A 級 verbatim）⭐⭐ 最殺差異化彈藥

### 直接證明「Claude Code 差異化」

1. **Microsoft Tech Community 用戶 verbatim**：「**I expect CoPilot to PERFORM tasks — not act like a manager that wants me to do their work.**」
2. 同 thread：「**Not only will it NOT place the content into my open word doc, but it can't even get the contents of the file correct.**」
3. 同 thread：「**Grok just works. ChatGPT just works. Claude just works. Copilot is a dumpster fire.**」
4. 評論者：「**Copilot has been a NEGATIVE productivity boost.**」
5. HBR Workslop — Finance IC：「**It created a situation where I had to decide whether I would rewrite it myself, make him rewrite it, or just call it good enough.**」
6. HBR Workslop — Tech Frontline Manager：「**It was just a little confusing to understand what was actually going on in the email and what he actually meant to say.**」
7. HBR Workslop — Retail Director：「**I had to waste more time following up on the information and checking it with my own research.**」
8. HBR Workslop 數據 verbatim：每次處理一份 workslop 平均花「**1 hour and 56 minutes**」
9. HBR Workslop verbatim：**53% 感到 annoyed / 38% confused / 22% offended**
10. Workplace Insight：「**workslop**」= 「**AI generated work content that masquerades as good work, but lacks the substance to meaningfully advance a given task.**」
11. Stack Overflow Blog 2026：「**Agents failed to deliver on the kind of utopia that was promised in 2025.**」

## 一、戰略與決策（10 條）

1. [戰略] 要不要接這張單卡好幾天 — 怕接了賠錢、不接被替代

3. [戰略] 新事業要不要 all-in — 沒人能幫你算清退路
4. [戰略] 老產品要砍但養活半個公司 — 砍了營收掉、不砍被淘汰
5. [戰略] 想轉型但不知道從哪轉 — 顧問報告看完還是一樣茫
6. [戰略] 二代要接班但方向跟老的不合 — 沒人敢當中間人
7. [戰略] 接到中國訂單要不要做 — 帳期、地緣風險自己扛

# 用法（給下游卡牌設計）

1. **A 區優先**：33 條 verbatim quote 是卡牌任務口語校準的主素材，每條都可標來源 URL，殺傷力最強
2. **線 3「AI 工具卡住」最關鍵**：直接證明 Claude Code「真做事 vs 給建議」的差異化，是卡背「ChatGPT 為什麼做不到」的彈藥
3. **B 區當地圖**：100 條分類覆蓋老闆所有工作面向，設計任務卡前用來檢查「有沒有漏到某個痛點類別」
4. **卡牌主素材篩選**：A 區 + B 區交集 = 既有真實 quote、又被分類覆蓋 = 最強的任務卡候選
